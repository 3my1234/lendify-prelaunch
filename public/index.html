About Lendify

Founded by Emmanuel Obaze, Lendify is pioneering a new approach to cryptocurrency. Unlike conventional cryptocurrencies, Lendi isn't meant to be hoarded - it's designed to be productive and create real-world value.

When you purchase Lendi, you're not just buying a digital token - you're investing in what it can produce: refineries, manufacturing facilities, and other industrial ventures that generate ongoing returns.

How Lendify Works

Pre-Sale

Early buyers can purchase Lendi at $1 = 2 Lendi, creating a financial pool of up to $500 million.

Investment

Funds are used to establish mini crude oil refineries and manufacturing industries in Africa and worldwide.

Transactions

As businesses are established, transactions occur in Lendi via our application.

Returns

Transaction fees are split between Lendify, early buyers, and businesses adopting Lendi as payment.
